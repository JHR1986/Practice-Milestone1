Group Training

All members are welcome to participate in our group exercise classes. Led by our personal trainer team, these classes are held 6 times a day Monday to Saturday and are a great way to get involved in the gym. The classes must be pre-booked and the PDF Timetable (button below) details all of the times. The class types on offer are listed below;

Class Options:

  • Cardio
  • Body Pump
  • Pilates & Yoga
  • Fitness Dance

Personal Training

To ensure you get the perfect gym plan that suits you and to start seeing results quickly, upon joining the gym you will be assigned a fully qualified trainer for our free 3 Step plan programme. On your first appointment your trainer will speak with you about the results you wish to achieve and will then guide you through how to use all of the equipment on your gym plan. Your trainer will make it simple and easy for you to understand how to use each piece of equipment.
