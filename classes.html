Group Training

Everybody is welcome to participate in our group exercise experience. Young or old. Fit or unfit. It’s all good. We set out to create a health club that’s different from all the rest. We love helping our members to be active. The welcome experience eliminates the worry of the unknown, our trainers will be on hand to show you all the equipment and technique that you need for the upcoming class. Any questions you may have they are on hand to answer and will ensure you get the most of your workout.

Class Options:

  • Les Mills Body Pump
  • Les Mills CX Work
  • Pilates
  • Yoga

Personal Training

To ensure you get the perfect gym plan and to start seeing results quickly you will be assigned to your fully qualified trainer for our free 5 step plan programme. On your first appointment your trainer will talk with you in detail about the results you want to achieve. Your trainer will then guide you step by step through the equipment on your gym plan. Your trainer will make it simple and easy for you to understand how to use each piece of equipment.

Class Options:

  • Les Mills Body Pump
  • Les Mills CX Work
  • Pilates
  • Yoga
